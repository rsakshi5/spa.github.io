Palak Paneer

Spinach or Palak in Hindi, is probably one of the highest sources of vitamins, minerals and anti-oxidants among food sources. It is a power veggie, which definitely should be included in your daily diet. Here are our 4 must-try palak recipes:

Ingredients

  1. 150 gm paneer
  2. 1 tablespoon ghee
  3. 1 red chilli
  4. 2 tablespoon onion
  5. 1/2 teaspoon ginger
  6. powdered salt as required
  7. 1/2 teaspoon kasoori methi powder
  8. 2 tablespoon fresh cream
  9. 200 gm spinach
  10. 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  11. 1 tablespoon garlic
  12. 1 teaspoon green chilli
  13. 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  14. 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  15. 25 gm butter
  16. 1 dash yellow chili powder

Steps to make delicious Palak Paneer

Step 1: Wash and boil the spinach for Palak Paneer

Step 2: Cook the spinach puree with milk and fresh cream

Step 3: Cook paneer in this spinach gravy and serve hot

Tips

For making Palak Paneer at home, use fresh spinach leaves instead of blanched ones. It has more nutrients than the one which is blanched.

To maintain that green colour of your Palak Paneer, make sure you use a little less garam masala powder as it brings a little brown colour to any dish.

For a silky smooth flavour in your Palak Paneer, you can add the puree of cashews or almonds (1-2 tsp) while cooking.

Try to use Homemade Paneer for this recipe as it would bring a freshness to your dish along with a smooth flavour.

Always soak the store-bought paneer for 20-30 minutes in warm water.
