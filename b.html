Ingredients

  1. 1/2 cup cheddar cheese
  2. 170 gm cream cheese
  3. 20 mushroom
  4. 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  5. 1 teaspoon extra virgin olive oil

Steps to make delicious Cheese stuffed Mushroom

Step 1~Take the bacon slices and cut them into 1/4-inch pieces. Cook these pieces in a skillet on medium flame until they are almost crispy.

Step 2~Now, take them out using a slotted spoon to help drain the fat. Place the bacon pieces on paper napkins in order to further drain it. Then, clean the mushrooms and discard their stems.

Step 3~Take a bowl and blend in softened cream cheese and shredded cheddar cheese. Also add the bacon you prepared in the beginning and minced garlic. Fill this mixture into the mushroom tops.

Step 4~Take a baking pan and grease it with a little olive oil. Place the mushrooms in it and bake them at 175 degree Celsius in an oven for approximately 25 minutes. The mushrooms should be tender once baked. Let them sit for 5 minutes and serve immediately!
